Operating Systems: Project 3

A Multilevel Scheduling Queue

Spring 2024 Jonathan Boyd

This assignment called for the implementation of a quasi-MLQ scheduler. The professor desired us to use a preset array of process values, I assume to allow for grading our math. Because of this, I didn't go about making the program profoundly dynamic. I believe I have done the proper math here. There was an interesting challenge in working with variable arrival times. All together, the experience was a great refresher on min-heap data structures and the loop body was very fun to create.
